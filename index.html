About Us

V-Pass 360° Advisory is a business financial consultancy which has been established to give specialized services through customer centered approach in addressing industry challenges. It serves as a strategic bridge for MSMEs that may lack access to high-end consultancy services from established advisory firms or lacks the capacity to invest in skilled, qualified talent.

We are keen in equipping businesses and its human capital through personalized training on a holistic approach in addressing business challenges and building a robust business strategy that is a cutting-edge in a competitive market environment.
